About

With a name based on the Sanskrit (आविष्कृत) word meaning ‘to reveal’ or ‘to make visible’, Aviskr seeks to spot patterns and discrepancies alike within the world of international affairs and foreign policy and make apparent through a visual means. Through a deep rooted, data driven analytical approach married with supplementary, nuanced policy analysis, Aviskr aims to embody the successful union of data and policy prescription.

Avsikr is inherently subscribed to presenting data in an interactive and engaging form, in order to truly resonate with the audience and start dialogue on current affairs at all levels for the wide spectrum that makes society.

A key element of this lies within a medium of communication which predates modern and ancient civilization – imagery. Merging the statistics and written analysis with engaging artistic work allows a greater depth of understanding and, most importantly, conveys the message we look to share in its simplest form: Signs and symbols rule the world, not words, nor laws.
